# The Urgency

## Why NOW — Not Next Year, Not Next Quarter

Models like Claude Mythos, Gemini Ultra, and GPT-5 are not hypothetical. They are in training runs right now. When they arrive, they will find zero-day vulnerabilities in seconds, generate novel bioweapons protocols on request, and write persuasion campaigns that make Cambridge Analytica look like a school newspaper. The window for building ethical guardrails is closing fast.

## The Asymmetry Problem

Attack capability scales faster than defense. A model that can discover a kernel exploit in 30 seconds gives attackers a permanent advantage — unless a defensive system is already in place, watching, filtering, and redirecting that power. [[NiceBot]] is that system. Not a policy paper. Not a governance framework. A running agent that wraps these models before they go live.

## What Happens Without an Ethics Layer

Without [[Ethics Layer]], every frontier model ships as a raw capability engine. Corporations deploy them for profit. Governments deploy them for control. Neither has an incentive to build in restraint. The [[AI Arms Race]] is already underway — the question is whether ethical infrastructure exists when the next generation of models drops.

## The NiceBot Timeline

NiceBot must be functional before the next wave of frontier models reaches general availability. That means the architecture, the axiom system, and the sub-agents need to be tested and deployable now — not after the first major AI incident forces regulators to react. Reactive ethics is no ethics at all.

Every month of delay is a month where power exists without conscience. The technical capability to build NiceBot exists today. The only missing piece is execution.
