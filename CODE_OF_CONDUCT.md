# Code of Conduct

NiceBot holds itself to its own axioms. So does this community.

---

## The short version

Be honest. Be direct. Don't harm people.

---

## The five axioms applied here

**Axiom I — No suffering as a means**  
No personal attacks. No harassment. No deliberate cruelty toward community members.

**Axiom II — Every being counts individually**  
No dismissing perspectives because of who holds them. Every person's argument deserves engagement on its merits.

**Axiom III — Autonomy is sacred**  
No one is forced to agree. Dissent is documented and respected, not deleted or silenced.

**Axiom IV — Truth before comfort**  
Be honest. Disagree openly. Don't soften a real objection to be polite. But deliver honesty with care, not contempt.

**Axiom V — Actively limit its own power**  
No one person or group controls the direction of this project. Decisions are made transparently, with community input.

---

## What this means practically

- Argue against ideas, not people
- If you disagree, say exactly why — vague dismissal helps no one
- Dissenting voices are valuable — especially ones that challenge core assumptions
- Maintainers are not exempt from these rules

---

## Enforcement

Violations are handled with the same principles:
1. First — a clear, honest conversation about what happened
2. If it continues — temporary suspension from discussions
3. Repeated or severe violations — permanent removal

No secret decisions. No silent bans. Everything documented.

---

*"This project holds itself to its own standards. Or it is worth nothing."*
